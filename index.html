Appraisal Questions For Congregations

Part 1: Community

1-1. Which statement best describes the growth or decline of our community?

1-2. Which statement best describes the economic status of our community?

1-3. Is there a significant change expected in our community that might have an impact on our population and/or economy?

1-4. Which statement best describes the changes in the demographic make-up of our community over the past ten years?

1-5. Which statement best describes the demographic make-up of our congregation compared to the make-up of our community?

1-6. Which statement best describes the religious nature of our community?

1-7. What is the driving distance from our congregation to the nearest LCMS congregation?

1-8. If our congregation were to close what are the majority of our current members most likely to do?

Part 2: Pastoral Care and Finances

2-1. Which statement best describes how our congregation is receiving pastoral care?

2-2. [If question 2-1 was answered “A”] What percentage of our district’s compensation guidelines are we currently providing in our pastor’s compensation?

2-3. [If question 2-1 was answered “B”] How many hours a week does our pastor work in a second occupation to supplement his income from the congregation?

2-4. How long has our pastoral care situation been what it now is?

2-5. How many pastors have served our congregation on an ongoing basis over the past ten years?

C) 3.

2-6. Over the past ten years, how many different sole or senior pastors (other than vacancy or interim pastors) have served our congregation?

2-7. How old is our current pastor?

2-8. Without borrowing any money or using a line of credit, how long could our congregation continue ministry if all contributions stopped today?

2-9. What is our status with Concordia Plans or another benefits provider?

2-10. What is our status on our mortgage or facility lease payments?

2-11. [If you answered 2-10 “C”, “D”, or “E”] Which action best describes our current situation?

2-12. Which of the following statements best describes our congregation’s debt status?

2-13. What is the status of our congregation’s line of credit?

2-14. How do this year’s weekly offerings (excluding special gifts) compare to last year’s weekly offerings?

D) Somewhat lower.

2-15. What percentage of our current budget is designated for supporting outside entities?

Part 3: Congregational Life

3-1. How does our total worship attendance compare to what it was 5 years ago?

3-2. How many local children have been baptized in our congregation during the past 3 years?

3-3. How many local children have been confirmed in our congregation during the past 3 years?

3-4. How many local adults have been baptized in our congregation during the past 3 years?

3-5. How many local adults have been confirmed in our congregation during the past 3 years?

3-6. How many funerals for local congregational members have we conducted during the past year?

3-7. On average, how many first-time local visitors by family unit have we had monthly during the past year?

3-8. Over the past year, what percentage of local first-time visitors returned for a second or subsequent visit?

3-9. What percentage of our current members has been members for 5 or more years?

3-10. On average, how many people attended weekly worship service during the past year?
